Shipping & Returns Policy

Shipping Information

We offer fast and reliable delivery across India. All orders are dispatched within 24–48 hours of confirmation.

  • Standard Delivery: 3–5 business days
  • Express Delivery: 1–2 business days (available for select locations)
  • Shipping Charges: Free on orders above ₹499; ₹50 for orders below ₹499

Return Policy

If you’re not satisfied with your purchase, we’re here to help. Products can be returned under the following conditions:

  • Return requests must be raised within 7 days of delivery
  • Items must be unused, undamaged, and in original packaging
  • To initiate a return, email us at shivduttkarwa@gmail.com

Refund Process

Once your return is approved, refunds are processed to your original method of payment within 5–7 business days.
