Our Roots

Farmizo started with a humble dream: to reconnect people with the earth and its purest offerings. Nestled in the fertile lands of Suratgarh, our farm has flourished into a symbol of sustainability, authenticity, and community-driven agriculture. What began as a family passion is now a movement towards organic, toxin-free living. Our farmers are the heartbeat of Farmizo, and together, we sow, nurture, and harvest with love and care.
