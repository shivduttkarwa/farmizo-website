About Farmizo

Welcome to Farmizo, your trusted partner in healthy living and organic lifestyle. Nestled in Suratgarh's fertile heartland, our farm brings you chemical-free, farm-fresh produce cultivated with love, tradition, and technology.

With a mission to reconnect people with nature, we practice sustainable agriculture that nourishes the soil and preserves biodiversity. Every tomato, spinach leaf, or mango from Farmizo is a testament to our dedication to purity and taste.

We believe organic food should be a right, not a luxury. That’s why we keep our prices honest and fair, support our community of local farmers, and work on awareness programs that teach the value of clean eating and conscious living.

Our modern supply chain ensures minimal waste and maximum freshness. Whether you order online or visit us at our weekend markets, you’ll taste the difference in every bite.

Join the Farmizo family today. Let’s sow the seeds of a healthier tomorrow, together.

More About Us

At Farmizo, transparency and community are at the heart of everything we do. We love opening our farm to local school children and curious visitors for guided tours, helping them learn firsthand how food is grown without synthetic inputs.

We also collaborate with eco-initiatives, host seasonal workshops on composting and kitchen gardening, and are developing tech-driven traceability so you can follow your food from seed to plate.

Beyond farming, Farmizo is about creating a movement. One where families eat clean, farmers earn better, and nature thrives. We’re more than a brand — we’re a conscious community.

Keep in touch through our newsletter, join us at pop-up organic markets, or follow our farming journey on social media. Thank you for believing in us and making Farmizo a part of your everyday life.
